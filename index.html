Creative Project 2 - Stock Data


Multiple stock results can be fetched (up to 3) at once by seperating symbols with commas, no spaces

Example Stock Symbols: Apple = AAPL , Microsoft = MSFT, Zions Bank = ZION, Tesla = TSLA

Example Crypto/ForEx Symbols: BTCUSD, USDGBP

Look up more symbols on Yahoo finance
